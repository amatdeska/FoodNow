How It's Works



Using FoodNow is as easy as 1..2..3



    1. Select a restaurant or type of food from the
      available categories and recomendations that
      GO-FOOD has personalised only for you.
    2. Or Enter the name of a restaurant or type of
      food you would like.
    3. Select the type and quantity of dish(es)you
      would like to order.
    4. Enter the delivery address and select the
      payment method, then click on Order.
    5. Your order is on it's way!.
